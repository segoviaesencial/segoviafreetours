Private tour in Segovia

Enjoy the wonderful city of Segovia on a private tour of two hours. You will walk through the historical streets learning about the monuments, legends and curiosities with a professional, experienced and local tour guide.

A private tour offers several advantages over a group tour

  1. Personalized Experience: You can customize the route to fit your interests, pace, and preferences. You get to spend more time on activities and sites that are interesting to you.
  2. Flexibility: With a private tour, you can make spontaneous changes to the schedule, allowing for a more relaxed and enjoyable experience.
  3. Exclusive Attention: You receive undivided attention from your guide, who can provide more in-depth information, answer your questions thoroughly, and cater to your specific needs.
  4. Comfort and Privacy: You can enjoy the tour in the company of your own group, without having to share the experience with strangers, leading to a more comfortable and intimate experience.
  5. Efficiency: Private tours can be more time-efficient, as there is no need to wait for other participants. You can maximize your time at each location and avoid unnecessary delays.
  6. Special Access: Sometimes private tours offer access to exclusive areas or special experiences that are not available to larger groups.

Overall, private tours provide a more tailored, comfortable, and flexible experience, making them ideal for travelers looking for a more personalized adventure.

Conditions of private tours

To reserve this type of visit, we provide the means for the visitor to tell us what type of visit they want to do (tour, date, number of people, time) and, depending on our availability, we will agree on a Price.

Private guided tours can be canceled free of charge up to 48 hours before the tour begins. If the client does not show up at the meeting point at the agreed time, the tour will not take place after 30 minutes and the Price of the private tour will not be refunded.

If, due to force majeure, it is not possible for us to carry out the private tour, the client will be informed as soon as possible, and the amount paid will be refunded in full.
